The difference

Watch it press a vague answer

Most practice tools accept whatever you say and move on. A real interviewer doesn’t, so this one doesn’t either.

Interviewer

Tell me about a time you disagreed with a technical decision and what you did about it.

You

Our team lead wanted to roll our own queue and I felt strongly we should use something managed, so I pushed back and eventually we aligned and the project worked out well.

Follow-up

You said it worked out well. What shipped, and what does ‘well’ look like in numbers?
