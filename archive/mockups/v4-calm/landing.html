How a session goes

01

Bring three things

Your CV, the role, and the job description. SoTellMe reads all three before it asks anything, so the questions are about your work, not a generic bank.

02

Have the conversation

One question at a time, spoken out loud. It follows up where a real interviewer would and gives you room to think, so you practice the version that counts.

03

Read the report

Afterward you get specifics: where a story ran long, where the impact went missing, and what to say differently in the room.
